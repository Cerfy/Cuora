Cuora's FAQs

This section contains a wealth of information, related to Cuora and about us. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us.



General Questions

Who are we?
We are a group of Computer Science students from Nanyang Technological University currently in our year 4, we built this website for the sake of answering frequently asked questions from Freshmen.
How did Cuora come about?
We noticed a big problem every year at a school welcome event that many of the freshmen do not know how certain components of the course work, example such as how are the GPA calculated, how many group projects does a certain module have and so we decided to build a website which address most (if not more) of such issues.
Do I need an account for this website?
No account registration at Cuora is needed. You can freely use the tools as you wish.
Is Cuora affliated to SCSE?
Yes, think of this website as an additonal website of the official SCSE website.
I can't find the information I'm looking for. Who do I contact?
Any enquires that the ChatBot fails to return you, please contact us at clement.tan.xian.ren@gmail.com and we'll be more than happy to answer them for you.

How does Cuorask work?
Whenever a question has been asked by a user, our Chat Bot will look for similarities in our system and return the relevant answer to the question.
